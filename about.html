About Oystiva

Growing nutrition. Inspiring health. Farming with purpose.

Our Story

Oystiva was born from a passion for sustainable farming and nutrient-rich foods. What started as a small oyster mushroom project has grown into a community-focused brand dedicated to offering natural, wholesome products.

Our Mission

To provide clean, healthy, and affordable mushroom-based products while empowering local farmers and promoting modern agriculture practices across Kenya.

Why Mushrooms?

Oyster mushrooms are packed with protein, antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. They support immunity, child development, and overall wellness — making them a superfood for families.

What We Produce

• Fresh Oyster Mushrooms
• dried mushroom
• Value-added mushroom snacks
• Training & consultation for new farmers

Our Values

Quality • Sustainability • Innovation • Community Impact

Our Vision

To become the leading oyster mushroom brand in Kenya at large, trusted for nutrition, quality, and sustainable farming.
